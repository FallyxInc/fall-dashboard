The KPI's of Our Analysis Tool


Learn about how we measure success in pilots of our fall/incident analysis & tracking tool to improve safety, save time, and ensure compliance.

Let's Partner for Success

We're excited to pilot with you and demonstrate the transformative value our tool delivers. Before we begin this journey together, let's align on what success looks like for your organization.

Measurable Outcomes

We've developed comprehensive metrics to track and measure the impact of our solution in your environment.

Data-Driven Success

Our key performance indicators are designed to demonstrate clear ROI and operational improvements.

Quantitative Metrics:

1.) The amount of time saved on incident tracking & auditing:

This is measured through surveys that we send out before and after the pilot begins which we craft with you. We would consider a 50 - 70% decrease in time spent on incident & fall analysis as success.

2.) Reduction in incidents of non-compliance:

This is specific to the type of home (LTC, IL, AL or Memory Care) and how compliance is currently measured in your homes.

Case Study: Scarborough Retirement Residences (SRR)

A 124-bed IL/AL home in Ontario using PCC saw improved falls tracking and increased adherence to RHRA & ORCA standards after implementing Fallyx.

Case Study: Responsive Long Term Care

A 15 home LTC Chain using PCC saw a 7% decrease in non-compliance metrics across 5 pilot homes, leading to full-scale implementation.

3.) Tool Adoption & Engagement

We track metrics such as:

  • Login frequency to Fallyx's dashboards
  • Usage of reports in care discussions
  • Utilization of graphs for analysis
  • Post-pilot adoption intention

Qualitative Metrics:

Through this data we're looking to understand the experince your wellness/nursing team has with our tool, as well as provide more insight that can help us drive conclusions. We gather this feedback through surveys including questions such as:


  • How helpful is the tool in automating monthly fall reports?
  • On a scale of 1-10, how much time does Fallyx's tool save in manual tracking & analysis?
  • How effective is the tool in identifying trends and patterns?
  • Has the data helped prevent future incidents?

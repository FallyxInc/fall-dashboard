The Problems

1. Undetected Resident Falls

$227,000 is the is the average cost for an undetected fall-related claim & 40.4% of all claims against retirement homes are fall-related. Currently, retirement homes don't have any way of automatically detecting falls because more than 80% of falls go undetected.

2. Manual Fall Data Analysis:

Retirement homes spend hours every month analyzing their previous fall data to create fall reports for compliance and to help with fall prevention. This process is extremely time consuming.

Fall Detection & Resident Monitoring


To solve the problem of undetected falls, we've created a waist-worn clip-on sensor to automatically detect falls.

Automatic Fall Detection

Each resident has a waist-worn sensor that captures motion and location data. This data is sent to an AI model to detect a fall. If a fall is detected, the sensor automatically calls the nurse’s handheld phone.

24/7 Location Monitoring

We monitor each resident’s location down to the specific room they’re in. If a fall alert is triggered, staff are notified of the resident's exact location, enabling immediate assistance.

3 Month Battery Life

Our sensor has a three month battery life, and we notify staff when it’s time for a replacement. Once the battery is below 10%, the staff can simply swap in a new coin cell battery. These batteries are provided by Fallyx.

Added Monitoring Features

Our sensors also have the ability to:
1.) Detect when the sensor is being worn or not.
2.) Perform wander management for each resident.

Fallyx's Fall Analysis Tool

Our team realized that your care staff is spending hours manually analyzing fall data and coming up with reports for compliance and analysis purposes. We created a tool that automatically does this for you.

1) Pulling Fall Data

Our tool securely gathers the fall data needed to fill out your falls tracking forms & answer questions you might have about your falls. Our AI algorithm looks at both your post-fall & fall notes to understand the context behind the fall.

2) Displaying the Data

We provide each home with a dashboard with details about each fall and a management dashboard to view summaries across the chain. Monthly reports with graphs are emailed, summarizing details like location, time, cause, etc.

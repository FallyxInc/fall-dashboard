The Problem

There's 2 main issues that retirement homes face which we help solve:


Undetected Resident Falls & Wandering:

$227,000 is the is the average amount paid for 1 undetected fall-related claim & 40.4% of all claims against retirement homes are fall-related. Currently, retirement homes don't have any way of detecting falls and the data shows this because more than 80% of falls go undetected.

Manual Fall Data Analysis:

Nursing managers & directors of care spend hours every month analyzing their fall data to make fall tracking reports for copmliance and to help them reduce falls. This is alot of manual labour that is wasted every month and is very time consuming.

Fall Detection & Resident Monitoring


Our team has worked on multiple fall detection systems from smartwatches to cameras to RADAR sensors. This combined expertise had led to us to create what we believe is the best fall detection system.

Waist Worn Clip-on Sensors

Each resident is given 1 waist worn clip-on sensor which captures motion & indoor location data to help us understand if & where a resident has fallen. The clip-on sensor also replaces your PERS system with the ability to tap it to call for help.

Gateways for Data Transmission

Throughout the home we place discreet gateways that receive data from our sensors and send them to a machine learning model that analyzes this data. These gateways use BLE/Bluetooth to connect with our sensors and send the data through Wi-Fi.

3 Month Battery Life

Our sensor has a 3-month battery life and we notify your staff when it needs to be changed. The battery replacement is simple and the same as the pendants, front-desk or admin staff just unscrew the bottom part of the casing and slip in a new coin cell battery.

Added Monitoring Features

Our sensors don't just detect falls, they also have the ability to:
1.) Detect when the sensor is being worn or not so that we can ensure it's effective.
2.) Monitor location 24/7, which can be helpful for wander management & activity tracking.

Fallyx's Fall Analysis Tool

Our team realized that your care staff is spending hours manually analyzing fall data and coming up with reports & tracking forms for compliance and analysis purposes. We created a tool that does all of this for you automatically.

25 homes

  • PIPEDA Compliant

1.) Pulling Fall Data

Our tool automatically logs into your EHR and securely gathers the fall data needed to fill out your falls tracking forms & answer questions you might have about your falls. Our AI algorithim looks at both your post-fall & fall notes to understand the context behind the fall so we can gather accurate information.

2.) Displaying the Data

We provide each home with a dashboard that displays their fall data, and management with a dashboard that allows them to see all their homes data. Every month our tool automatically emails each home their fall tracking forms & reports filled out, along with quarterly reports for management.
